I'm Adrian, a front-end developer! My love for coding began with open-source Android development and a passion for creating useful content. Realizing that I could turn this passion into a career, I took a few classes and found an organization for veterans, Vets Who Code. Completing this bootcamp program opened my eyes to what I could achieve and provided a solid foundation for coding. After grooming my skills in Git, HTML, and CSS (and SMACSS), I started learning Javascript and JAMstack, followed by other technologies such as React, Gatsby and Jest. I enjoy creating content that allows me to learn and share my creative abilities.
